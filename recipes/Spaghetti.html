How to make Spaghetti

Restaurant-worthy spaghetti sauce with ground beef is just a few steps away.

Spaghetti Ingredients

  1. 1 pound ground beef
  2. 1 medium onion, chopped
  3. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  5. 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  6. 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  7. 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  8. 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  9. 2 teaspoons dried basil
  10. 1 teaspoon of salt
  11. 1/2 teaspoon ground black peeper

How to make Spaghetti Step-by-step

step 1

Gather all ingredients

step 2

Combine ground beef, onion, garlic and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.

step 3

Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste into the pan. Season with Oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

There you have it, you have made your own 5 star spaghetti!
